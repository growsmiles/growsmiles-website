Our Mission

Grow Smiles is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to promoting oral health education, environmental sustainability, and community service. Through educational initiatives, service projects, outreach campaigns, and donation drives, we aim to create healthier smiles and healthier communities while inspiring students to become leaders and changemakers.

Why It Matters

3.5 Billion+

People worldwide are affected by oral diseases, many of which can be prevented through education, awareness, and access to proper care.

8 Million+

Tons of plastic enter oceans each year, reminding us that protecting our enviornment is essential for building healthier communities and a healthier future.

90%

Most oral diseases are preventable through proper hygiene, education, and regular dental care.

Our Goal

Grow Smiles was founded on the belief that even small actions can create meaningful change. Our goal is to educate, inspire, and empower communities through oral health awareness, and environmental stewardship, and service.

We want to help indivduals gain access to valuable health education, encourage healthier habits, and provide students with opportunties to make a real difference in their communitites. Every project, volunteer, and initiative brings us one step closer to creating a future where healthier smiles and stronger communities go hand in hand.

Together, we are turning passion into purpose and service into impact.
